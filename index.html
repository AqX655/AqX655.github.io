About Me

I am a PhD at School of Mathematical Sciences, Tongji University. I obtained my PhD in Applied Mathematical, Tongji University.

Research Interests

I am broadly interested in computational problems arising from signal processing, machine learning and data science, with an emphasis on the design and analysis of efficient numerical algorithms via tools from numerical linear algebra, optimization, probability and statistics.

Research areas of current focus include high dimensional signal processing and data analysis, reinforcement learning, and nonconvex optimization. For those who are interested in working with me, feel free to drop me an email.
